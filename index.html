About

Mohammadi Pasha — Founder & CEO

I'm a full-time trader and financial advisor with over 7 years of experience in the markets.

My journey began in 2019 in the Indian stock market, focusing on Nifty, intraday, and swing trading. What started as curiosity evolved into a disciplined pursuit — built through continuous screen time, real losses, and consistent refinement.

In 2021, I expanded into global markets, specialising in forex and commodities. My core focus includes XAUUSD (Gold), USOIL, BTCUSD (Bitcoin), and XAGUSD (Silver), with a strong emphasis on price action, liquidity, and institutional order flow.

Beyond trading, I developed expertise in GST and income tax filing under Government of India regulations, strengthening my foundation in financial advisory.

Today, through Finances Vision, I help individuals not only trade better but also manage their finances with structure — covering investment planning, retirement strategies, and budgeting systems.

Over the years, I've worked with 1000+ clients across India, guided by one principle:

"Protect capital first. Build consistency next. Scale with discipline."

TradingView Indicator

Price Action Indicator

1) Identifies market structure using higher highs/lows and lower highs/lows.
2) Highlights key support and resistance zones based on price behavior.
3) Helps track trend direction and potential breakout or reversal areas.
4) Commonly used for both intraday and swing trading setups.

ICT / SMC Indicator

1) Focuses on smart money concepts like liquidity, order blocks, and market structure shifts.
2) Identifies areas where institutions are likely to enter or exit positions.
3) Highlights potential reversal zones using imbalance and liquidity sweeps.
4) Used to refine entries with better risk-to-reward positioning.
